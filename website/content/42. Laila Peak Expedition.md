Expedition Overview
Laila Peak is one of the most striking mountains in the world.
Its near-perfect spear-shaped summit rises dramatically above the glaciers of the Hushe Valley and has become an icon of Pakistan's mountain landscapes.
Laila Peak
Known for its elegant summit pyramid and steep snow-covered ridges, Laila Peak attracts climbers from around the world seeking a technical alpine challenge in one of the most beautiful corners of the Karakoram.
For photographers and mountaineers alike, Laila Peak represents one of the ultimate climbing objectives in Pakistan.

Frequently Asked Questions
Is Laila Peak technical?
Yes. While lower than many major expedition peaks, it requires solid alpine climbing skills.

Is Laila Peak suitable for beginner climbers?
No. Previous mountaineering experience is recommended.

Can Laila Peak be combined with Gondogoro La Trek?
Yes. This is a popular combination for experienced adventurers.

How long does the expedition take?
Typically 18–25 days.

What is the best climbing season?
June through September.
