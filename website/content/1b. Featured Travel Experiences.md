Follow the Ancient Silk Road
For centuries, the Silk Road connected civilizations across Asia, facilitating trade, cultural exchange, and exploration.
Today, travelers can retrace sections of these legendary routes through Pakistan's northern mountains, ancient caravan settlements, Buddhist heritage sites, and spectacular landscapes.
Our Silk Road experiences include:
Pakistan Silk Route Tours
Journey along the historic Karakoram Highway, one of the world's most spectacular mountain roads, passing through Gilgit, Hunza, Passu, and Khunjerab Pass.
Buddhist Heritage & Gandhara Tours
Explore ancient monasteries, archaeological sites, and Buddhist heritage locations that once flourished along the trade routes connecting South Asia, Central Asia, and China.
Karakoram to Central Asia Journeys
Experience the cultures, landscapes, and history that link Pakistan with the broader Silk Road world.
Multi-Country Silk Road Adventures
Specially designed journeys connecting Pakistan with selected destinations in Central Asia, creating truly unique travel experiences.
