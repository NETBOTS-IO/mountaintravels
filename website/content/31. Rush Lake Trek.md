Trek Overview
The Rush Lake Trek is one of Pakistan's most scenic and rewarding trekking adventures.
Located in the spectacular Nagar region of Gilgit-Baltistan, this trek leads travelers through traditional mountain villages, alpine meadows, glaciers, and high mountain landscapes before reaching the magnificent:
Rush Lake
Rush Lake is often cited among the highest alpine lakes in the world and offers extraordinary panoramic views of some of the Karakoram's most famous peaks.
For travelers seeking a challenging yet achievable trekking experience, the Rush Lake Trek provides an ideal balance of adventure, culture, and scenery.

Frequently Asked Questions
Is the Rush Lake Trek difficult?
It is considered a moderate trek suitable for reasonably fit travelers.

Do I need previous trekking experience?
Not necessarily, though hiking experience is beneficial.

How many days does the trek take?
Typically between 7 and 10 days.

Is Rush Lake really one of the world's highest lakes?
Yes. It is frequently listed among the world's highest alpine lakes.

Can I combine the trek with Hunza Valley?
Absolutely. Most itineraries naturally incorporate Hunza and Nagar.
