Discover the Mountain Kingdom of Pakistan
Home to the World's Greatest Mountain Landscapes
Gilgit-Baltistan is one of the most spectacular mountain regions on Earth.
Situated at the crossroads of the Karakoram, Himalaya, and Hindu Kush mountain ranges, it is a land of towering peaks, ancient Silk Road routes, turquoise lakes, dramatic valleys, historic forts, glaciers, and some of the warmest hospitality in Asia.
Home to five of the world's fourteen peaks above 8,000 meters, including K2, Gilgit-Baltistan attracts travelers, photographers, trekkers, mountaineers, historians, and cultural explorers from around the globe.
Whether you seek breathtaking scenery, cultural discovery, Silk Road history, or adventure, Gilgit-Baltistan offers unforgettable experiences unlike anywhere else on Earth.

Where is Gilgit-Baltistan?
Located in northern Pakistan, Gilgit-Baltistan borders:
China 
Afghanistan 
The region covers some of the most dramatic mountain terrain in Asia and serves as the gateway to the legendary Karakoram Highway and the ancient Silk Road.

The Geography of Gilgit-Baltistan
Few regions on Earth can match the geographical drama of Gilgit-Baltistan.
The region contains parts of:
Karakoram Range
Home to K2 and many of the world's highest peaks.

Himalaya Range
Including the Nanga Parbat region.

Hindu Kush Range
Extending toward Chitral and Central Asia.

Together, these mountain systems create one of the most remarkable landscapes on Earth.

The Silk Road Legacy
For centuries, Gilgit-Baltistan formed a vital section of the Silk Road.
Merchants traveling between:
China 
Central Asia 
South Asia 
passed through the valleys and mountain passes of the region.
Today, visitors can still experience traces of this remarkable history.

Frequently Asked Questions
Is Gilgit-Baltistan worth visiting?
Absolutely. Many travelers consider it one of the most beautiful mountain regions in the world.

How many days should I spend in Gilgit-Baltistan?
Most visitors spend between 7 and 14 days, although longer stays are highly rewarding.

What is the best time to visit?
Spring and autumn are particularly popular, while summer is ideal for trekking and mountain travel.

Is Gilgit-Baltistan suitable for cultural travelers?
Yes. The region combines stunning scenery with rich history, Silk Road heritage, and unique mountain cultures.

Can I combine Hunza and Skardu in one trip?
Yes. This is one of the most popular travel itineraries in Pakistan.
