Everything You Need to Know About Visiting Naran and Kaghan Valley
Discover Lakes, Meadows, Rivers and Mountain Passes in Northern Pakistan
Nestled in the mountains of Khyber Pakhtunkhwa, Naran and Kaghan Valley are among Pakistan's most famous tourist destinations.
Known for their alpine lakes, lush meadows, rushing rivers, snow-covered peaks, forests, waterfalls, and scenic mountain roads, these valleys attract thousands of visitors every year.
Whether you are planning a family holiday, honeymoon, photography tour, road trip, camping adventure, or nature excursion, Naran Kaghan offers unforgettable experiences in every season.

Frequently Asked Questions
What is Naran famous for?
Naran is famous for Lake Saif-ul-Malook, Babusar Pass, beautiful scenery, and summer tourism.

What is the best time to visit Naran?
Generally from May to October.

Is Naran suitable for families?
Yes. It is one of Pakistan's most popular family tourism destinations.

Can Naran be combined with Hunza or Skardu?
Yes. Many travelers continue through Babusar Pass toward Gilgit-Baltistan during summer.

How many days are required?
Four to five days are usually sufficient for most visitors.
