Complete Khaplu Travel Guide
Everything You Need to Know About Visiting Khaplu, Baltistan
Discover the Cultural Heart of Baltistan
Nestled among the spectacular mountains of Baltistan, Khaplu is one of Pakistan's most beautiful and culturally rich destinations.
Known for its centuries-old heritage, traditional Balti culture, historic palaces, ancient mosques, stunning mountain scenery, and proximity to some of the Karakoram's most famous trekking and mountaineering regions, Khaplu offers a unique blend of history, culture, and adventure.
Located in the Ghanche District of Gilgit-Baltistan, Khaplu serves as an important gateway to Hushe Valley, Masherbrum, Gondogoro La, and numerous trekking and climbing expeditions.

Where Is Khaplu?
Khaplu
Khaplu is located in eastern Baltistan along the banks of the Shyok River.
It lies approximately:
103 km from Skardu 
6–7 hours from Skardu by road (depending on conditions) 
Near the Pakistan-China and Pakistan-India frontier regions 
Khaplu is the administrative center of Ghanche District and one of the most important cultural centers of Baltistan.

Top Attractions in Khaplu

1. Khaplu Palace
Khaplu Palace
The most famous attraction in Khaplu.
Built in the 19th century, the palace served as the residence of the Raja of Khaplu and is now one of Pakistan's finest heritage restoration projects.
Highlights
✓ Traditional Balti architecture
✓ Historical exhibits
✓ Cultural heritage
✓ Beautiful gardens
✓ Luxury heritage accommodation
✓ Mountain views

2. Chaqchan Mosque
Chaqchan Mosque
One of the oldest mosques in Gilgit-Baltistan.
Dating back several centuries, the mosque reflects a unique blend of Tibetan, Persian, Kashmiri, and Central Asian architectural influences.
Highlights
✓ Historical significance
✓ Unique architecture
✓ Cultural heritage
✓ Religious importance

3. Khaplu Old Town
Explore the traditional streets and historic neighborhoods surrounding the palace area.
Highlights
✓ Traditional Balti houses
✓ Local culture
✓ Historic atmosphere
✓ Photography opportunities

4. Shyok River
Shyok River
The Shyok River adds to the dramatic beauty of the Khaplu region.
