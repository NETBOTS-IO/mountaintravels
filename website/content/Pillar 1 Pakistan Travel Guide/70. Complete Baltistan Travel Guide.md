Complete Baltistan Travel Guide
Everything You Need to Know About Visiting Baltistan, Pakistan
Explore the Land of Mountains, Glaciers, Lakes and Ancient Culture
Located in the heart of Gilgit-Baltistan, Baltistan is one of the most spectacular mountain regions in the world.
Home to towering peaks, vast glaciers, historic forts, alpine lakes, ancient villages, and a rich cultural heritage, Baltistan offers unforgettable experiences for travelers, trekkers, photographers, mountaineers, and nature lovers.
The region contains some of the world's most famous mountain landscapes, including K2, the Baltoro Glacier, Concordia, Deosai National Park, and the valleys of Skardu, Shigar, Khaplu, and Hushe.

Where Is Baltistan?
Baltistan
Baltistan lies in northeastern Pakistan and forms a major part of Gilgit-Baltistan.
The region borders:
Ladakh to the east 
Gilgit to the west 
The Karakoram Mountains to the north 
Baltistan is renowned for its dramatic mountain landscapes and unique Balti culture.
