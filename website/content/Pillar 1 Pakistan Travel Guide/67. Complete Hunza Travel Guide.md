Where Is Hunza Valley?
Hunza Valley
Hunza Valley is located in the northernmost part of Pakistan within Gilgit-Baltistan.
The valley lies along the historic Karakoram Highway and forms part of the ancient Silk Road connecting South Asia with China and Central Asia.
Hunza is approximately:
100 km from Gilgit 
600 km from Islamabad by road 
Near the Pakistan-China border

Frequently Asked Questions
Is Hunza safe for tourists?
Hunza is widely regarded as one of Pakistan's most welcoming and popular tourism destinations.

What is the best month to visit Hunza?
April for blossoms and October for autumn colors are among the most popular periods.

Can Hunza be visited year-round?
Yes, each season offers unique attractions and experiences.

How do I reach Hunza?
Via the Karakoram Highway from Islamabad or through Gilgit.

Is Hunza suitable for families?
Yes. Hunza is one of Pakistan's best family-friendly destinations.
