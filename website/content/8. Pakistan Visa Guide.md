Planning a Trip to Pakistan?
One of the first questions most travelers ask is:
"Do I need a visa to visit Pakistan?"
The answer is yes for most nationalities, but Pakistan has significantly simplified its visa procedures in recent years, making travel easier than ever before.
Many travelers can now apply online through Pakistan's electronic visa system, while certain nationalities may also be eligible for Visa on Arrival for tourism purposes.
This guide provides general information about Pakistan's visa process, helping you prepare for a smooth and hassle-free journey.
Important: Visa regulations may change periodically. Travelers should always verify the latest requirements through official government sources before making travel arrangements.

Do I Need a Visa for Pakistan?
Most foreign nationals require a visa to enter Pakistan.
Fortunately, Pakistan has introduced an online visa system that has greatly simplified the application process for many travelers.
Depending on your nationality, you may be eligible for:
✓ Electronic Visa (e-Visa)
✓ Tourist Visa
✓ Visa on Arrival (eligible countries)
✓ Business Visa
✓ Diplomatic or Official Visas
The appropriate visa type depends on your nationality and travel purpose.

Applying for a Pakistan e-Visa
Pakistan's online visa system has made the application process considerably easier.
The general process includes:
Step 1 – Prepare Required Documents
Typical documents may include:
Passport 
Passport-sized photograph 
Travel itinerary 
Hotel information 
Supporting travel documents 
Requirements vary by nationality and visa category.

Step 2 – Complete Online Application
Applicants submit their information through Pakistan's official visa platform.
Ensure that all information matches your passport details accurately.

Step 3 – Upload Supporting Documents
Required documents are uploaded electronically during the application process.

Step 4 – Pay Applicable Fees
Visa fees vary depending on nationality and visa category.

Step 5 – Await Processing
Processing times vary according to nationality, season, and application volume.
Applicants are encouraged to apply well in advance of their travel dates.

Passport Requirements
Visitors should generally ensure that their passport:
✓ Is valid for at least six months beyond their planned stay.
✓ Contains sufficient blank pages.
✓ Matches all information provided in the visa application.
Travelers should verify current requirements before submitting applications.
