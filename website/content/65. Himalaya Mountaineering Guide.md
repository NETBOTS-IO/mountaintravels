About Nanga Parbat
Nanga Parbat is one of the world's most famous mountains and one of the fourteen 8,000-meter peaks.
Its immense size, dramatic relief, and challenging climbing history have made it a legendary objective among mountaineers.
The mountain is often referred to as:
"The Killer Mountain."

The Famous Faces of Nanga Parbat

Rupal Face
Rupal Face
The Rupal Face is widely recognized as one of the highest mountain faces on Earth.
Its enormous vertical rise makes it one of the most impressive sights in world mountaineering.

Diamir Face
Diamir Face
The Diamir side provides access to one of the mountain's best-known climbing approaches.
