Grand Pakistan Tour
The Ultimate Journey Through Pakistan's History, Culture & Landscapes
21 Days | Private & Tailor-Made | Year-Round Departures
Experience the very best of Pakistan on a comprehensive journey that combines ancient civilizations, UNESCO World Heritage Sites, vibrant cities, spectacular mountain scenery, living cultures, and historic Silk Road routes.
The Grand Pakistan Tour has been carefully designed for travelers seeking an in-depth exploration of one of Asia's most fascinating and rewarding destinations.
From the bustling streets of Lahore and the archaeological treasures of Taxila to the breathtaking valleys of Hunza and the majestic mountains of Gilgit-Baltistan, this tour reveals the extraordinary diversity that makes Pakistan unique.
Whether your interests include history, architecture, culture, photography, archaeology, landscapes, or authentic local experiences, the Grand Pakistan Tour provides an unforgettable introduction to Pakistan.

Suggested Itinerary
Day 1 – Arrival in Lahore
Arrival and welcome assistance.
Transfer to hotel.
Depending on arrival time, enjoy an introductory orientation of Lahore.
Overnight in Lahore.

Day 2 – Lahore Heritage Tour
Explore:
• Lahore Fort
• Badshahi Mosque
• Walled City
• Shalimar Gardens
• Lahore Museum
• Food Street
Evening visit to the Wagah Border Ceremony.
Overnight in Lahore.

Day 3 – Lahore to Islamabad
Drive to Islamabad via the historic Grand Trunk Road.
Optional visits en route.
Explore Islamabad and Rawalpindi.
Overnight in Islamabad.

Day 4 – Taxila & Gandhara Heritage
Full-day excursion to Taxila.
Visit:
• Taxila Museum
• Dharmarajika Stupa

• Sirkap

Overnight in Islamabad.

Day 5 – Islamabad to Swat Valley
Journey to Swat Valley.
Enjoy scenic mountain landscapes and cultural experiences.
Overnight in Swat.

Day 6 – Explore Swat
Visit:
• Buddhist Heritage Sites
• White Palace
• Mingora
• Local Villages
Overnight in Swat.

Day 7 – Swat to Chitral
Travel through dramatic mountain scenery to Chitral.
Overnight in Chitral.

Day 8 – Kalash Valleys
Excursion to:
• Bumburet
• Rumbur
Meet local communities and learn about Kalash traditions.
Overnight in Chitral.

Day 9 – Chitral Exploration
Explore Chitral Town and surrounding areas.
Visit:
• Chitral Fort

Overnight in Chitral.

Day 10 – Chitral to Gilgit
Journey through spectacular mountain landscapes.

Day 11 – Gilgit to Hunza
Travel along the Karakoram Highway.
Visit:
• Rakaposhi View Point
• Historic Settlements

Overnight in Hunza.

Day 14 – Hunza to Skardu
Scenic journey through the mountains.
