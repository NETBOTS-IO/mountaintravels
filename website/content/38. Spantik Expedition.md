Expedition Overview
Spantik Peak is one of Pakistan's most popular mountaineering objectives and one of the finest introductory 7,000-meter expeditions in the Karakoram.
Known internationally as:
Spantik
the mountain rises gracefully above the Nagar and Shigar regions of Gilgit-Baltistan and is famous for its beautiful golden glow during sunrise and sunset.
For climbers aspiring to progress toward higher Himalayan and Karakoram objectives, Spantik offers the perfect combination of:
✓ High altitude
✓ Technical mountaineering
✓ Glacier travel
✓ Expedition experience
✓ Exceptional summit views

Why Is It Called Golden Peak?
The mountain's upper slopes often reflect brilliant golden light during sunrise and sunset.
This remarkable phenomenon inspired its famous nickname:
Golden Peak
The mountain is considered one of the most photogenic peaks in Pakistan.
