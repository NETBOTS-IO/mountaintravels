The Five 8000-Meter Peaks of Pakistan
Pakistan is home to five of the world's fourteen 8,000-meter mountains.
K2
The world's second-highest mountain and one of mountaineering's ultimate challenges.

Nanga Parbat
Known as the "Killer Mountain" and one of Pakistan's most famous peaks.

Broad Peak
A classic 8,000-meter expedition in the Baltoro region.

Gasherbrum I
One of the world's great high-altitude objectives.

Gasherbrum II
Often considered one of the more accessible 8,000-meter expeditions.
