Expedition Overview
Towering directly above Karimabad and Hunza Valley, Ultar Sar is one of Pakistan's most challenging and visually spectacular mountains.
The mountain rises dramatically from the valley floor, creating one of the most striking mountain landscapes in the Karakoram.
Ultar Sar
Renowned for its steep faces, technical ridges, and formidable climbing challenges, Ultar Sar is considered one of the most respected alpine objectives in Pakistan.
For experienced climbers, an ascent of Ultar Sar represents a significant achievement in high-altitude mountaineering.

Is Ultar Sar technical?

Is Ultar Sar suitable for intermediate climbers?
Generally no. Extensive mountaineering experience is recommended.

How long does the expedition take?

What is the best climbing season?
