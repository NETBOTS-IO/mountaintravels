Who Issues Climbing Permits?
Climbing permits are issued by the relevant government authorities responsible for mountaineering and adventure tourism in Pakistan.
Applications typically involve:
✓ Expedition registration
✓ Team member documentation
✓ Passport information
✓ Route details
✓ Expedition schedule
✓ Insurance information
✓ Environmental commitments

How Far in Advance Should You Apply?
We recommend beginning the permit process:
3–6 Months Before Departure
This allows sufficient time for:
Documentation preparation 
Government processing 
Corrections if required 
Travel planning 
Visa arrangements 
Logistics coordination 
For major expeditions such as K2, earlier preparation is highly recommended.
