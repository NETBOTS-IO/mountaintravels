What Are High Altitude Porters?
High Altitude Porters (HAPs) are experienced mountain professionals who assist climbing expeditions above Base Camp and on high mountain routes.
Their responsibilities may include:
✓ Carrying expedition equipment
✓ Transporting food and fuel
✓ Moving camp supplies
✓ Supporting route establishment
✓ Assisting camp operations
✓ Helping maintain expedition logistics
High Altitude Porters play an essential role in many successful mountaineering expeditions in Pakistan.
