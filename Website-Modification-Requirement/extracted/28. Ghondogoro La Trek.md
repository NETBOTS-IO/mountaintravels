Trek Overview
The Gondogoro La Trek is widely regarded as one of the most spectacular trekking routes in the world.
Combining the legendary K2 Base Camp Trek with the dramatic crossing of Gondogoro La Pass, this extraordinary journey takes trekkers through the heart of the Karakoram Mountains and across one of Pakistan's most famous high-altitude passes.
The trek provides access to:
K2 
Broad Peak 
Gasherbrum I 
Gasherbrum II 
Masherbrum 
The crossing of Gondogoro La reveals one of the most celebrated mountain panoramas anywhere on Earth.
For many experienced trekkers, this is the finest trek in Pakistan.

Frequently Asked Questions
Is Gondogoro La harder than K2 Base Camp?
Yes. The pass crossing makes it significantly more challenging than the standard K2 Base Camp Trek.

Do I need mountaineering experience?
Not necessarily, but previous high-altitude trekking experience is strongly recommended.

Is the route safe?
With proper planning, experienced guides, and suitable weather conditions, the trek is conducted safely.

How high is Gondogoro La?
Approximately 5,585 meters.

What is the best month for the trek?
July and August generally provide the most reliable conditions.
