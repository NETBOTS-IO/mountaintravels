For more than two thousand years, the Silk Route linked the great civilizations of Asia, the Middle East, and Europe.
Across deserts, mountains, fertile valleys, and bustling trading cities, merchants, monks, explorers, and travelers exchanged goods, ideas, religions, technologies, and cultures that shaped the world we know today.
From the ancient cities of Central Asia to the legendary Karakoram Highway and the mountain kingdoms of northern Pakistan, the Silk Route remains one of humanity's most fascinating travel experiences.
