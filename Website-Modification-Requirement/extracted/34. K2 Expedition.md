Expedition Overview
The K2 Expedition represents one of the greatest challenges in mountaineering.
Rising to 8,611 meters above sea level, K2 is the second-highest mountain on Earth and is widely regarded as the most difficult and demanding of all the 8,000-meter peaks.
Known throughout the climbing world as:
K2
and often referred to as "The Savage Mountain," K2 demands exceptional physical conditioning, technical climbing ability, high-altitude experience, and mental resilience.
For elite climbers, standing on the summit of K2 is considered one of mountaineering's ultimate achievements.
