Expedition Overview
Broad Peak is one of the world's fourteen 8,000-meter mountains and one of Pakistan's most celebrated climbing objectives.
Located on the border region of Pakistan and China, Broad Peak rises directly opposite K2 and forms part of the spectacular mountain landscape surrounding Concordia and the Baltoro Glacier.
Standing at:
Broad Peak
the mountain offers climbers the opportunity to experience the challenges of an 8,000-meter expedition while climbing one of the most beautiful peaks in High Asia.
Broad Peak is often considered one of the more accessible 8,000-meter mountains, though it remains a serious and demanding expedition requiring excellent preparation and experience.

Frequently Asked Questions
Is Broad Peak easier than K2?
Generally yes, but Broad Peak remains a serious 8,000-meter expedition requiring extensive preparation.

Can Broad Peak be combined with K2?
Yes. Many climbers attempt both mountains during the same season.

Do I need previous 8,000-meter experience?
Not necessarily, but strong high-altitude experience is highly recommended.

How long does the expedition take?
Typically 40–55 days.

What is the best time to climb?
July generally provides the best summit opportunities.
