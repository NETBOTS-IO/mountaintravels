The Hindu Kush is one of Asia's great mountain systems, stretching across northwestern Pakistan and neighboring Afghanistan.
Known for its remote valleys, towering peaks, traditional mountain cultures, and outstanding exploration opportunities, the Hindu Kush offers a very different experience from the Karakoram and Himalaya.
For climbers seeking less crowded mountains, authentic cultural encounters, and true wilderness adventures, the Hindu Kush remains one of the world's most rewarding mountain regions.

About Tirich Mir
Tirich Mir is the highest mountain in the Hindu Kush and one of Pakistan's most important expedition peaks.
Visible from parts of Chitral, its immense snow-covered summit dominates the surrounding landscape.
For many mountaineers, climbing Tirich Mir represents one of the greatest expedition experiences in the Hindu Kush.
