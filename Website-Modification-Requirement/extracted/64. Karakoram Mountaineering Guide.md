Karakoram Mountaineering Guide
The Complete Guide to Climbing in the Karakoram Mountains
Explore the World's Greatest High-Altitude Mountain Range
The Karakoram is one of the most spectacular mountain ranges on Earth and is widely regarded as one of the world's premier destinations for mountaineering and high-altitude exploration.
Stretching across northern Pakistan and neighboring regions, the Karakoram is home to some of the highest, most challenging, and most beautiful mountains ever climbed.
From the legendary slopes of K2 to the remote glaciers of Baltistan and the dramatic peaks of Hunza, the Karakoram offers extraordinary opportunities for climbers, trekkers, researchers, photographers, and adventure travelers.
Mountain Travels Pakistan has supported expeditions and mountain adventures throughout the Karakoram since 1990.

Famous Karakoram Glaciers
The Karakoram contains some of the world's largest non-polar glaciers.

Baltoro Glacier
One of the most famous glaciers on Earth and the approach route for K2, Broad Peak, and the Gasherbrums.

Batura Glacier
One of the longest glaciers outside the polar regions.

Hispar Glacier
Part of the famous Hispar-Biafo glacier system.
