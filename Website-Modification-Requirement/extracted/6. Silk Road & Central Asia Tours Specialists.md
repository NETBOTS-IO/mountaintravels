For more than two thousand years, the Silk Road connected civilizations, cultures, religions, and empires across Asia.
Merchants, pilgrims, explorers, scholars, and adventurers traveled these legendary routes, carrying goods, ideas, technologies, and traditions between East and West.
Today, the ancient Silk Road remains one of the world's most fascinating travel experiences.

Pakistan and the Silk Road
Pakistan played a vital role in the history of the Silk Road.
Ancient trade routes crossed the mountains of Gilgit-Baltistan, linked South Asia with Central Asia and China, and facilitated the movement of merchants, pilgrims, and explorers for centuries.
Today travelers can follow many of these historic routes while enjoying some of the world's most spectacular mountain scenery.
